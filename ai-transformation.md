## Where Work Breaks Down

Before applying AI, we classify why execution slows down:

### AUTOMATE

**When work slows down because humans are doing unnecessary manual tasks**

- Repetitive, rules-based effort
- High volume, low judgment
- Creates backlogs and errors

*Examples: Document extraction, straight-through processing, validation steps*

### AUGMENT

**When work slows down because humans lack good decision support**

- Incomplete or fragmented information
- Manual analysis
- Slow or inconsistent decisions

*Examples: Scoring, recommendations, drafting first outputs*

### CONNECT

**When work slows down because systems and data don't talk to each other**

- Manual reconciliation
- Partial visibility
- Slow handoffs across teams

*Examples: Cross-system matching, unified views, data stitching*
