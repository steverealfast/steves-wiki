## Two-Bucket Business Model

### Bucket 1: Salesforce (Vertical)

**Built-in distribution through the Salesforce channel**

- Accelerated implementations with smaller teams
- Fixed-bid pricing (outcome-based, not T&M)
- Faster time-to-value through AI-native delivery
- Leverage: SF AE relationships, partner program, Agentforce wave

### Bucket 2: AI Transformation (Horizontal)

**Enterprise AI services across any industry**

- Automate / Augment / Connect framework
- Safe starting doors: backlogs, latency, cost pressure
- 3-6 week delivery to production
- Proof before scale model
